About

Words that convert, not just impress

I write UGC-style ad scripts built around one goal: making people stop, watch, and buy. Every script follows a proven retention structure — hook, problem, solution, proof, CTA — tailored to the specific platform and audience.

My scripts are grounded in real audience research: competitor ad analysis, product reviews, Reddit threads, and social listening. The result is copy that sounds like your customer's inner monologue, not a marketing brief.

I specialize in beauty, haircare, wellness, and lifestyle brands — including Hinglish scripts for Indian millennial and Gen-Z audiences on TikTok and Instagram Reels.
